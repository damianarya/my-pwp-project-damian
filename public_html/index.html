Meet the team

Father Steve

Yes he is a real catholic priest. Father Steve is a former marine and currently in training to become a full blown exersitst. He was personal friends with Lorain Warren and has worked with many celebritys who have had paranormal encounters.

Patrick

Pat will do anything to get a thrill, ANYTHING. He grew up in a house where seances where a weekly occurrence. Pat specializes in tarot readings, photograghy and scaring his friends just for fun.

Chuy

a.k.a. the Mexicutioner. Chuy is our sound guy, he sets up the audio and filters it to hone in on the faint wispers we would otherwise miss. Chuy grow up in a haunted house and has been exposed to the paranormal his whole life.

Jeromy

The video expert of our team. He sets up the cameras and combs over the footage to catch anything we might not have seen. He also tends to wonder off on his own and make us all panic.

Damian

The spooky one. When the chips are down and the catholic mojo just isn't working everyone turns to Damian. He specializes in the occult including non-traditional exorcizm and spiritual protection.

Nick, the hipster

Ghost hunting is a lot more fun when your doing it with someone who is easily scared, that is why we bring Nick.

This is the site of the longest and bloodiest prison riot in U.S. history.

The La Verde in Socorro N.M. has a long and gruesome history.
