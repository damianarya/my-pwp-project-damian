meet the team

Father Steve, yes he is a real catholic preist. Father Steve is a former marine and currently in training to become a full blown exersitst. He was personal friends with Lorain Warren and has worked with many celebritys who have had paranormal encounters.

Patrick the team leader. Pat will do anything to get a thrill, ANYTHING. He grew up in a house where seances where a weekly occurrence. Pat specializes in tarot readings, photograghy and scaring his friends just for fun.

Chuy a.k.a. the Mexicutioner. Chuy is our sound guy, he sets up the audio and filters it to hone in on the faint wispers we would otherwise miss. Chuy grow up in a haunted house and has been exposed to the paranormal his whole life.

Jeromy is the vidio expert of our team. he sets up the camras and combs over the footage to catch anything we might not have seen. He also tends to wonder off on his own and make us all panic.

Damian, the spooky one. when the chips are down and the cathlic mojo just isnt working everyone turns to Damian. He specializes in the occult including non-traditional exorcizm and spiritual protection.

Nick, the hipster. Ghost hunting is alot more fun when your doing it with someone who is easily scared, thats why we bring Nick.
